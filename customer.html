Delivery service made for you.

Why dasp?

Created for you

Our whole system is designed and developed to be fully adaptable to you. Each segment of the experience is tailored to your needs, from the beginning to the end. The timeframe of the delivery, delivery man, price - everything is chosen by you.

Price

Most of the people are accustomed to fixed prices for services that are often too high. But this is not the case with DASP. Once you put up an order, Daspers send you their offers, and you choose the one that suits all your needs.

Free time

What we provide to our customers is not solely a delivery service. We offer them an opportunity to have more free time every day by assigning mundane and unnecessary chores to our Daspers. We hope you value your free time as much as we do.

Behind the scene

Care how it's done?

Once you're done with the clicking and ordering the real magic happens. Daspers, packages, the voyage. Everything is like a piece of a watch - precise and synchronised. Daspers are our delivery men who are ordinary people just like everyone, willing to deliver and move the world onwards. They bid for your offer, move your packages, pick up groceries for you, wait in line to buy your goods. And then hastily deliver them to you. Daspers are available 24/7 and if you like how a specific Dasper does his job you can favourise him to stay in touch for further deliveries.

Our mission

Problem

We value your free time, and we know that you dont like doing mundane chores and time consuming unnecessary tasks like picking up groceries from the store, going to the fast food joint to get your favourite grub or delivering some packages over the whole city while you could do some more productive stuff.

Solution

Our idea is that we brign you an on-demand delivery service at your fingertips that is available 24/7 but with a catch! The price - just like the whole experience - is adjusted to you and only you! Your personal delivery man - Dasper - is yours to be! Scroll down to see how its done.

Process

1. Order

All you need to do is place your order and wait for a quick response from a buch of Daspers all willing to get you some free time

2. Choose

Once the bids from Daspers come rolling in you choose the one you like based on the price, projected time frame of the delivery or the reputation of the Dasper

3. Enjoy

Its all done, just like that! You can relax and enjoy your free time while your Dasper buzzes around doing your tasks. After all, its your time, istnt it?

We create free time

Our mission

Problem

We value your free time, and we know that you don't like doing mundane chores and time consuming unnecessary tasks like picking up groceries from the store, going to the fast food joint to get your favourite grub or delivering some packages over the whole city while you could do some more productive stuff.

Solution

Our idea is that we brign you an on-demand delivery service at your fingertips that is available 24/7 but with a catch! The price - just like the whole experience - is adjusted to you and only you! Your personal delivery man - Dasper - is yours to be! Scroll down to see how it's done.

Process

1. Order

Place your order for anything you need

2. Choose

Choose the Dasper that suits your needs

3. Enjoy

Sit back and relax, enjoy your free time
